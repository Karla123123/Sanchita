The Scoop

Devastatingly feminine, dangerously flirtatious - this blend of white flowers should be a controlled substance. If you love gardenia, you need to own this - it is so incredibly fresh and true that it smells like you just pinned a newly cut gardenia in your hair. Lush, but clean. It is an oil, so remember: a little bit goes a long way. The tiniest dab should have people leaning in when they speak to you.

Kai - Perfume Oil Fragrance Notes

Notes of gardenia and white exotic florals
